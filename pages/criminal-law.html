Powerful Defense

Criminal Law & Cyber Defense

Vigorous advocacy in white-collar and technology-driven criminal matters.

Protecting Your Liberty & Reputation

Facing criminal charges is a life-altering experience. Shan Ali provides expert legal defense with a particular focus on the intersection of law and technology. In an age where digital evidence often determines the outcome of a case, our expertise in cyber forensics and PECA (Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act) litigation is an invaluable asset.

We handle everything from FIA inquiries and white-collar fraud investigations to complex criminal trials, ensuring that our clients' constitutional rights are protected at every stage of the legal process.

PECA & Cybercrime Defense

Defending against charges under the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016. We handle cases involving unauthorized access, digital forgery, and online defamation.

White-Collar & Commercial Crimes

Strategic representation in cases of commercial fraud, embezzlement, and money laundering investigations before specialized agencies.

FIA Inquiry Defense

Navigating inquiries by the Federal Investigation Agency (FIA). We provide early-stage legal counsel to prevent inquiries from escalating into formal charges.

Post-Arrest & Pre-Arrest Bail

Urgent representation for securing protective, pre-arrest, and post-arrest bail in the Sessions and High Courts.
