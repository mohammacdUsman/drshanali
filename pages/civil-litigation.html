Strategic Dispute Resolution

Civil disputes can be a significant drain on time and resources. Shan Ali provides vigorous representation across all levels of Pakistan's judiciary, specializing in complex commercial and civil matters. Our approach combines meticulous research with powerful oral advocacy to achieve the best possible outcomes for our clients.

From breach of contract claims to property entanglements, we represent both plaintiffs and defendants with a focus on strategic resolution, whether through courtroom litigation or alternative dispute resolution (ADR) mechanisms like arbitration and mediation.

Commercial & Contractual Disputes

Litigating high-stakes commercial breaches, including partnership disputes, vendor conflicts, and service agreement failures.

Property & Land Litigation

Expertise in declaratory suits, specific performance claims, and partition disputes before civil courts and revenue authorities.

Arbitration & ADR

Handling domestic and international arbitration proceedings. We specialse in the enforcement of foreign arbitral awards in Pakistan.

Constitutional Writs

Challenging administrative overreach and protecting fundamental rights through writ petitions before the Islamabad High Court.
