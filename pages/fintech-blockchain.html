Expertise

FinTech & Blockchain Law

Navigating the regulatory complexities of the digital financial revolution.

Advanced Legal Solutions for Emerging Tech

As the landscape of financial technology evolves, so do the legal challenges associated with its implementation. Shan Ali provides cutting-edge legal advisory for startups and established enterprises operating in the FinTech and Blockchain space, ensuring compliance with both local and international regulatory frameworks.

In Pakistan, the regulatory environment is rapidly changing with the introduction of regulatory sandboxes by the SECP and State Bank. Shan Ali helps clients navigate these experimental frameworks while maintaining high standards of governance.

Regulatory Sandboxes & Licensing

Guidance on applying for and operating within SECP's regulatory sandboxes. We assist in obtaining Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) licenses and ensuring adherence to the latest SBP regulations.

Smart Contract Legal Audits

Translating code into legal clarity. We perform legal audits of smart contracts to ensure they align with contract law, property rights, and consumer protection statutes.

Token Economics & ICO/STO Compliance

Structuring Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) and Security Token Offerings (STOs) to comply with securities laws. We provide legal opinions on token classification (Utility vs. Security).

VASP Governance

Developing internal governance protocols for digital asset exchanges and payment service providers, focusing on board liability and operational risk management.
