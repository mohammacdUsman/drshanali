Strategic Integrity & Compliance

In an era of increased global surveillance and FATF scrutiny, Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) compliance are no longer optional—they are the bedrock of institutional trust. Shan Ali provides expert MLRO (Money Laundering Reporting Officer) services and designs robust compliance frameworks tailored to the unique risks of tech-enabled finance.

We bridge the gap between regulatory requirements and operational efficiency, ensuring your business stays ahead of sanctions, anti-bribery laws, and evolving financial crime patterns.

MLRO Services & Reporting

Acting as or advising your designated Money Laundering Reporting Officer. We handle the submission of Suspicious Transaction Reports (STRs) and ensure adherence to local regulatory reporting timelines.

KYC/CDD Framework Design

Designing risk-based Customer Due Diligence (CDD) and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) processes. We help automate onboarding while maintaining strict legal compliance.

Sanctions & PEP Screening

Implementing legal protocols for screening against global sanctions lists (OFAC, UN, EU) and Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs) databases.

Compliance Audits & Training

Conducting independent AML audits and providing certified training sessions for your staff to foster a culture of compliance and prevent accidental breaches.
