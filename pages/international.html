Global Expertise

International Legal Research &
Cross-Border Expertise

Shan Ali's legal career spans multiple continents and jurisdictions. From research fellowships at the University of Zurich (UZH) to delivering blockchain law education programs in Dubai and attending international conferences in Italy, the UK, and Germany — he brings a truly global perspective to every legal matter.

His international research interests include Decentralised Autonomous Organisations (DAOs), AI governance, blockchain regulation in the EU and UK, cross-border AML/KYC frameworks, and the legal implications of DeFi systems.
