Meet Shan Ali,
Blockchain & FinTech Legal Counsel

Shan Ali is a distinguished legal professional specialising in FinTech, Web3, and Blockchain Law. He is a PhD Fellow in Blockchain Law, licensed attorney before the Islamabad High Court, and a prolific scholar with over 13 peer-reviewed publications in leading international journals.

"The intersection of technology and law is not a challenge — it is an opportunity to build a more equitable digital future."

With experience spanning Anti-Money Laundering (AML), DeFi compliance, cross-border corporate law, and international research fellowships at institutions across Europe, Shan Ali brings a truly global perspective to every legal matter.
